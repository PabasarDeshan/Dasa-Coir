ABOUT US

OUR HERITAGE

Our Heritage

Over four decades of dedication to the coir industry.

Dasa Coir was established in 1975 as a small company manufacturing coco peat and coir fibre using the traditional milling process. From these humble beginnings, we have grown into a cornerstone of the industry in Sri Lanka.


In 1990, recognizing the potential of the international market, we expanded our operations to become a sub-contractor for major exporters, specializing in twisted fibre and fibre bales.


The turn of the millennium marked a significant milestone. In the year 2000, Dasa Coir became a direct exporter. Today, we take pride in delivering a range of high-quality coco peat and coir fibre products to clients across the globe, ensuring that the natural excellence of Sri Lankan coir reaches every corner of the world.

Our Core Values
